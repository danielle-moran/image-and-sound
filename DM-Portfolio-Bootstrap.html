Welcome to my portfolio site!

I am a first-year, part-time journalism student in the data concentration at Columbia J School. By day I work full time as a public finance reporter for Bloomberg News while by night taking classes to hone my skills. I'm basically a very nerdy, not cool at all, super hero.

Subjects I have reported on include:

Puerto Rico Debt Crisis

Puerto Rico's debt crisis continues as the governor's office released a roadmap plan that show's the hurricane ravaged island will have a deficit before paying any debt service payments to investors.

Louisville Basketball Scandal

The University of Louisville relies so heavily on its basketball program, the NCAA recruiting scandal, which already cost the program it's hall of fame coach, may now peril the University's financial status.

Tax Plan and Rural Hospitals

Critical access hospitals in rural areas of America are already in peril. The House of Representatives proposed GOP tax bill would have made operating them, that much harder - causing many to close their doors.
